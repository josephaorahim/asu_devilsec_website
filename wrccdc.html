--- layout: default --- # Western Region Cybersecurity Defense Competition (WRCCDC)

A Blue Teaming competition organized across US. Competitors are asked to perform different bussiness tasks like creating a FTP Server, Scanning the network, adding a domain user, while being attacker by Red Team (Skilled Hackers). The competition is divided into 10 regions, e.g., Western, Mid West. Winning team from each region participated in National Cybersecurity Defense Competition (NCCDC)
